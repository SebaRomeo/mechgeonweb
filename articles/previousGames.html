Mech-Geon Legacy

This was the only project that I "finished". Was my thesis for the university, a third person shooter where the map is generated procedurally like in The Binding of Isaac, the player must go through different rooms, defeat every enemy in the room in order to get random rewards and then fight the final boss of the level. If you are interested you can check gameplay here.



This legacy version, as the new one too, are developed in Unity Engine.
